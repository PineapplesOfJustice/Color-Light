~ Just Some Facts About Light ~

Facts:

  • To us, light is truly invisible, unless it hit a cloud of particles or get reflect off of a hard object.
  • Light can be absorped, reflected, and/or transmitted according to the vibration frequency of the electrons.
  • Light is what we perceived as colors.
  • Using a prism, light can be splitted into seven colors and observed. These colors are red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, and violet.
  • These colors each have their own frequency with red as the lowest and violet as the highest.
  • White is all the colors, whereas black is nothing. This is because black absorped all of the colors so our eyes can't pick up light.
  • Visible light is only a small part of the electromagnetic spectrum.
  • Visible light is the only part of the electromagnetic spectrum that we can perceived.
  • Each type of elements have a tendency to absorp a specific wavelength.
  • Light don't stop, it keep going on for eternity until it is absorped and converted into heat energy.
  • We have three type of cone cells in our eyes which perceived a different range of frequency each.
  • The electromagnetic spectrum was named for its electric-like and magnetic-like properties.
  • Light move as a wave-particle duality.
  • Light is still clouded with mysteries.
